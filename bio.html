Biography

Megs Yadav

She was born in Milwaukee, WI and has lived in Seattle, WA; Tampa,FL; and Naples,FL (where she currently lives). She now attends Northwestern University where she is doing the BS/MS program in McCormick with a BS in Computer Engineering and a MS in Computer Science. She is also completing the Kellogg Undergraduate Business Certificate in Managerial Analytics. She has had 2 internships at Arthrex, a Biomedical Device Manufacturing Company, in their IT Department and an internship at Boeing in their Propulsion Department. This upcoming summer, she will be interning at Boeing again in their Avionics Department with the Flight Management Divsion. Some of her hobbies include long distance running (she hopes to run a marathon by the time she is 25 years old) and watching football.

Outside of class, she is involved in many organizations on campus. She is a tour guide for Northwestern Admissions, the Vice President of the Electrical and Computer Engineering Honor Society, HKN, where she leads the mentorship program, a mentor for the Society of Women Engineers, a member of the Electronics subteam of Aero NU, and a member of the Delta Gamma sorority.
